Frequently Asked Questions

What is your hourly rate?
For all services, I start at $25 per hour.
What services do you offer?
I offer logo creation, social media graphic content, and web design. If there is something else you are looking for, please contact me and I would love to work something out!
How long does a project typically take to complete?
This will vary greatly! For projects where I start from scratch, this will take longer than a project that has preexisting content. Additionally, I want to make sure that I create exactly what you envisioned and better! This may require going back and forth for review. A contract will be written at the beginning of service, and if the client requests more than originally outlined on the contract, this will result in more hours and pay.
Where are you located?
I am located in Indianapolis, IN, but I offer my services anywhere in the US! Since this content lives online, our communication and meetings can as well. For all clients that are close to the Indidnapolis area, I would love to sit down with a cup of coffee and discuss your hopes and dreams with my creative service.
